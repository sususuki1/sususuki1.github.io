The Fish and the Bird

By Rabindranath Tagore

"The fish in the water is silent,
The animal on the earth is noisy,
The bird in the air is singing,
But Man has in him the silence of the sea,
The noise of the earth and
The music of the air."

On Love

By Rabindranath Tagore

"Love is life in its fullness like the cup with its wine.

When love is not madness, it is not love.

The same love that makes me laugh makes me cry.

Let life be beautiful like summer flowers
and death like autumn leaves."

On Life and Death

By Rabindranath Tagore

"The world has kissed my soul with its pain,
asking for its return in songs.

The touch of the nameless days clings to my heart
like mosses round the old tree.

Death's stamp gives value to the coin of life;
making it possible to buy with life what is truly precious."

My Favorite Songs

Discover some of my favorite songs that have inspired and accompanied me through different stages of life. Each song is selected for its lyrical depth, melodic beauty, and emotional resonance.

Love Story

Taylor Swift from Fearless (2008)

A timeless love song that reimagines Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet with a happy ending. The song's catchy melody and romantic narrative made it a breakthrough hit for Taylor Swift, blending country and pop elements seamlessly.

Cruel Summer

Taylor Swift from Lover (2019)

A vibrant pop anthem that captures the intensity of a summer romance. With its infectious beat and relatable lyrics about love's highs and lows, "Cruel Summer" has become a fan favorite, showcasing Taylor's ability to evolve her sound while maintaining emotional depth.

You Belong With Me

Taylor Swift from Fearless (2008)

A classic teenage love song that tells the story of unrequited love. The music video, featuring Taylor in dual roles, became an iconic part of her early career, and the song's relatable lyrics about friendship and love have made it a timeless favorite.

Always Online

JJ Lin (林俊杰) from JJ陆 (2008)

A touching ballad that explores the theme of enduring love and connection. JJ Lin's soulful vocals and the song's beautiful melody have made "Always Online" a classic in Chinese pop music, resonating with listeners through its message of love that transcends distance and time.
